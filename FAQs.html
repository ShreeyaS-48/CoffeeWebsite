Frequently Asked Questions

  • My Coffee Cards

    You can download the mobile app and add a e-Card to your registered account. You can also buy physical Cards from any store. You can use your Card to purchase any item like - Food, Beverage, Retail coffees, Ready to Drink, Ready to Eat, Merchandise, etc .at all stores.

  • My Coffee Rewards

    When you log into your account online or on mobile app, you will be able to see all the rewards you have earned at your level when you click on the tier strip at the first screen of app or you can scan your MSR card at any store and Barista will tell you the status of your rewards. This recognition is presented in the form of stars or rewards earned.

  • Order Through Call

    Orders can be placed 1 hour after the store opens and 1 hour before the stores close for the day.
